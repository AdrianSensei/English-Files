A Slow and Careful Morning

Today I wake up early, but I move slowly. I do not want to make noise, because my little brother is still sleeping. I walk quietly to the kitchen and make breakfast carefully. I pour the milk slowly so I do not spill it. My mother smiles and says I am very helpful. After breakfast, I brush my teeth quickly but well. Then I pack my bag neatly and check my homework carefully. I walk to school slowly and enjoy the cool air. It is a calm and careful morning, and I feel ready for the day.
