In My Living Room

My living room is small, but I like it. The big sofa is in front of the TV, and a soft rug is on the floor. A small table is between the sofa and a chair. My school bag is under the table, and my books are on the table. There is a green plant next to the window. Our family photo is on the wall, and a clock is above it. Sometimes our cat sleeps behind the sofa. At night, I sit on the sofa and read a book in this quiet room. It feels warm and safe.
