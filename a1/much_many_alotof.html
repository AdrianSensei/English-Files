Much / Many / A lot of

量を表す語。「たくさんの」を意味します。

What do they mean?

We use “much,” “many,” and “a lot of” to talk about large quantities.

「much」「many」「a lot of」は大量を表すときに使います。

Usage

Use “many” with countable nouns. Use “much” with uncountable nouns. “A lot of” works with both.

「many」は可算名詞、「much」は不可算名詞に使います。「a lot of」はどちらにも使えます。

Expression Use Example
many Countable nouns many books
much Uncountable nouns much water
a lot of Both a lot of people / a lot of rice

Examples

She has many friends.
日本語を見る / 隠す 彼女にはたくさんの友達がいます。

He doesn’t drink much milk.
日本語を見る / 隠す 彼は牛乳をあまり飲みません。

We have a lot of homework today.
日本語を見る / 隠す 今日は宿題がたくさんあります。

Notes

“Much” is rare in positive sentences; we usually use “a lot of.”

肯定文では「much」はあまり使われず、「a lot of」を使うことが多いです。

Exercises

1. Choose the correct word

much / many / a lot of を入れましょう。

a) She has ___ books. 答えを見る many

b) I don’t have ___ time. 答えを見る much

c) They have ___ money. 答えを見る a lot of

2. Translate into English

英語にしましょう。

a) 私はたくさんの友達がいます。 答えを見る I have many friends.

b) 彼らはお金をあまり持っていません。 答えを見る They don’t have much money.

Mini‑Quiz

1) 「彼はたくさんの本を持っています。」

a) He has much books.
b) He has many books.
答えを見る b) He has many books.

2) 「私はたくさんの水を飲みます。」

a) I drink a lot of water.
b) I drink many water.
答えを見る a) I drink a lot of water.
