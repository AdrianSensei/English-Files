Past Simple — Questions

過去の疑問文。「〜しましたか？」を表します。

How do we make questions?

Use **Did + subject + base verb?**

疑問文は **Did + 主語 + 動詞の原形** を使います。

Form

Positive Question Meaning
You watched a movie. Did you watch a movie? あなたは映画を見ましたか。
She went to school. Did she go to school? 彼女は学校へ行きましたか。

Important

Use the **base verb** after “Did.”

“Did” の後は必ず動詞の原形。

✖ Did you went?
→ ✔ Did you go?

Examples

Did you study yesterday?
日本語を見る / 隠す あなたは昨日勉強しましたか。

Did he eat breakfast?
日本語を見る / 隠す 彼は朝ごはんを食べましたか。

Did they see the movie?
日本語を見る / 隠す 彼らはその映画を見ましたか。

Short Answers

Question Yes No
Did you go? Yes, I did. No, I didn’t.
Did she study? Yes, she did. No, she didn’t.

Exercises

1. Make questions

疑問文にしましょう。

a) You played soccer. 答えを見る Did you play soccer?

b) She ate sushi. 答えを見る Did she eat sushi?

c) They went to Kyoto. 答えを見る Did they go to Kyoto?

2. Translate into English

英語にしましょう。

a) あなたは昨日映画を見ましたか。 答えを見る Did you watch a movie yesterday?

b) 彼女は宿題をしましたか。 答えを見る Did she do her homework?

Mini‑Quiz

1) 「彼らは昨日学校へ行きましたか。」

a) Did they go to school yesterday.
b) Did they went to school yesterday.
答えを見る a) Did they go to school yesterday.

2) 「あなたは朝ごはんを食べましたか。」

a) Did you eat breakfast.
b) Did you ate breakfast.
答えを見る a) Did you eat breakfast.
