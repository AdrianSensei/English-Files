Present Continuous — Questions

現在進行形（疑問文）。「〜していますか？」を表します。

How do we make questions?

Put am / is / are at the beginning of the sentence.

文の最初に am / is / are を置きます。

Form

Use am / is / are + subject + verb-ing.

am / is / are + 主語 + 動詞の ing 形を使います。

Subject Question Form Example
I Am I + verb-ing? Am I studying?
he / she / it Is + subject + verb-ing? Is she reading?
you / we / they Are + subject + verb-ing? Are they playing?

Examples

Are you studying now?
日本語を見る / 隠す あなたは今勉強していますか。

Is he eating lunch?
日本語を見る / 隠す 彼は昼ごはんを食べていますか。

Are they running in the park?
日本語を見る / 隠す 彼らは公園で走っていますか。

Short Answers

Use “Yes, I am.” / “No, I’m not.” or “Yes, he is.” / “No, he isn’t.”

短い答えは「Yes, I am. / No, I’m not.」などを使います。

Question Yes No
Are you studying? Yes, I am. No, I’m not.
Is she reading? Yes, she is. No, she isn’t.

Exercises

1. Make questions

疑問文にしましょう。

a) You are watching TV. → 答えを見る Are you watching TV?

b) She is cooking dinner. → 答えを見る Is she cooking dinner?

c) They are studying English. → 答えを見る Are they studying English?

2. Translate into English

英語にしましょう。

a) 彼は今走っていますか。 答えを見る Is he running now?

b) あなたたちは宿題をしていますか。 答えを見る Are you doing homework?

Mini‑Quiz

1) 「彼女は勉強していますか。」

a) Is she studying?
b) Are she studying?
答えを見る a) Is she studying?

a) Are they playing?
b) Is they playing?
答えを見る a) Are they playing?
