Past of be — was / were

be 動詞の過去形。「〜でした／〜でしたか／〜ではありませんでした」。

What are “was” and “were”?

They are the past forms of the verb *be*.

「was」「were」は be 動詞の過去形です。

Form

Use “was” for I / he / she / it. Use “were” for you / we / they.

I / he / she / it → was、you / we / they → were を使います。

Subject Past form Example
I / he / she / it was She was happy.
you / we / they were They were tired.

Negative Form

Use “was not (wasn’t)” and “were not (weren’t).”

否定文は「was not / were not」を使います。

Questions

Put “Was / Were” at the beginning.

疑問文は「Was / Were」を文頭に置きます。

Form Example Meaning
Was + subject…? Was he at home? 彼は家にいましたか。
Were + subject…? Were they busy? 彼らは忙しかったですか。

Examples

I was tired yesterday.
日本語を見る / 隠す 私は昨日疲れていました。

They were at school.
日本語を見る / 隠す 彼らは学校にいました。

She wasn’t happy.
日本語を見る / 隠す 彼女は幸せではありませんでした。

Exercises

1. Complete the sentences

was / were を入れましょう。

a) I ___ at home yesterday. 答えを見る was

b) They ___ very busy. 答えを見る were

c) She ___ not at school. 答えを見る was

2. Translate into English

英語にしましょう。

a) 彼は昨日忙しかったです。 答えを見る He was busy yesterday.

b) あなたたちは家にいませんでした。 答えを見る You were not at home.

Mini‑Quiz

1) 「彼女は昨日家にいました。」

a) She was at home yesterday.
b) She were at home yesterday.
答えを見る a) She was at home yesterday.

2) 「彼らは疲れていませんでした。」

a) They weren’t tired.
b) They wasn’t tired.
答えを見る a) They weren’t tired.
